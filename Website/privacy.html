Jobory Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026. This policy explains what information Jobory may collect, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

1. Information you provide

You may enter customer names, phone numbers, email addresses, service addresses, job titles, dates, prices, notes, job status, and customer tags into Jobory. Jobory does not store this business data on servers owned or operated by us.

2. Location information

Jobory may use location-related data on your device to show job addresses on maps, support navigation, and help you plan your workday. Jobory does not store your location data on servers owned or operated by us. You can control location permissions in iOS Settings.

3. App data and CloudKit

Your Jobory data is stored on your device and, when sync is available or enabled, in Apple CloudKit/iCloud. We do not operate a separate backend server for storing your customer, job, schedule, route, or income data. Apple account, iCloud, and CloudKit behavior are governed by Apple's terms and privacy policies.

4. Analytics and diagnostics

Jobory may collect limited diagnostic or usage information to improve reliability, understand feature performance, and fix bugs. This information is used to operate and improve the app.

5. Purchases and subscriptions

If Jobory offers paid features or subscriptions, payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. Jobory does not receive your full payment card number.

6. Support communications

If you contact support, we may receive your email address, message content, screenshots, device information, and any other information you choose to share so we can respond to your request.

7. How information is used

  • To provide customer, job, schedule, route, and income features.
  • To save app data on your device and sync it through Apple CloudKit/iCloud when supported.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To maintain security, troubleshoot issues, and improve Jobory.

8. Sharing

Jobory does not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers needed to operate the app, when required by law, or with your direction or consent.

9. Data retention

Your business records remain on your device and Apple CloudKit/iCloud according to your app usage and Apple account settings. You may delete app records within Jobory where available.

10. Your choices

You can update or delete records in the app, manage notification and location permissions in iOS Settings, and manage subscriptions through your Apple ID settings.

11. Children's privacy

Jobory is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, the effective date at the top of this page will be updated.
